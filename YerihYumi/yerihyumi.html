ABOUT US

Yerih Yumi Laundry Shop was founded by Jaquelyn G. Benocilla
on July 5, 2019. The shop currently operates with nine efficient machines.
We're legally registered with a Mayor's Permit, BIR taxpayer ID,
and DTI business name registration for reliable and compliant service.

ADDRESS

Yerih Yumi Laundry Shop is located in 221 Patola St. brgy. BF International Village
CAA. In order to reach the shop, from CAA road, you need to enter Green Revolution St.,
which will lead you to the CAA Elementary School- Main. You then need to turn left
and then right, and proceed directly straight forward intil you see phase 3 half-court.
Yerih Yumi Laundry Shop can be found between phase 3 half-court and Nit's store.
